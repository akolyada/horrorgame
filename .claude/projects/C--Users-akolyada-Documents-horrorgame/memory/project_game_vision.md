## Game Concept: Abandoned Kindergarten Horror

**Setting**: Покинутий дитячий садок (abandoned kindergarten/daycare)

**Monster concept**: Monsters are created from children's drawings that have come to life — very creepy, Poppy Playtime inspired. Models will be created from actual drawings (the user's son draws them).

**Gameplay**: Escape from AND kill monsters. Not just stealth — combat is part of it.

**Map**: One large map with multiple rooms (interconnected kindergarten building).

**Scare design**: Jump scares + creepy atmosphere. Both active frights and passive dread.

**Visual style**: Realistic and dark, inspired by Poppy Playtime. Dark lighting, abandoned environment with remnants of a children's space (toys, drawings, small furniture).

**Top priorities (from the creator):**
1. Proper scary monster models
2. Sound design (footsteps, creaking, breathing)
3. Multiple rooms with doors
4. Dark atmosphere

**Why:** This is a father-son project. The son is the creative director — he designs monsters and guides the vision. Communication is in Ukrainian.

**How to apply:** All implementation decisions should serve this vision. Prioritize atmosphere and the monster experience over technical complexity.
