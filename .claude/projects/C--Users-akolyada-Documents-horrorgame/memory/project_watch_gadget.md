## Годинник-помічник (Helper Watch)

**Look**: Toy children's watch (like something from a kindergarten). Colorful but worn/cracked — it's old and abandoned like everything else in the садок.

**Features:**
1. **Flashlight** — Small, weak beam. Limited visibility = more fear. Attached to the watch, so it points where the player looks.
2. **Monster detector** — Beeps/vibrates when monsters are nearby. Beeping gets faster as they get closer (like a Geiger counter). Visual indicator on the watch face.
3. **Navigation hints** — Gradually shows text hints about where to go next. Acts as the objective/quest system.

**No battery mechanic mentioned** — flashlight is always on (weak by design, not by resource).
**No voice/AI** — just beeps and text on screen.

**How to apply:** The watch replaces a standard flashlight. It's the player's only tool and UI device. HUD elements (monster proximity, objectives) should be diegetic — shown on/around the watch rather than floating UI where possible.
